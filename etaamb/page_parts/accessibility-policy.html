etaamb.be accessibility policy

This website endeavours to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines Version 2.0 (WCAG 2.0) at a minimum "Double-A" (AA) rating, and providing a high quality user experience to the end users.

However, due to the experimental, "side-project" nature of etaamb.be, total compliance with the WCAG 2.0 guidelines is not and can not be guaranteed.
